About

Hi! We're "Sinisters", sisters Jenn & Steph, who love to make fun, flirty sentiments for you! We have been creating art together forever! Our art is inspired by our awesome hometown of Louisville & all the people who live here, visit, or are just passing through. You can celebrate everyday like it's a holiday with handmade art, cards & gifts. Sinisters can add a lil' sugar 'n' spice to your everyday life!

Jennifer Meyers is a fine artist/portraitist with a BFA of Fine Arts from the Art Academy of Cincinnati. www.jennmeyers.com

Stephanie Meyers is a graphic designer with a BFA of Fine Arts from Northern Kentucky University. www.designgreenlight.com
